EDA On Indian Cars

Tools:

Scikit-Learn,Juypter Notebook, Python,numpy , pandas


This project focuses on Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) of Indian cars using Jupyter Notebook and Python libraries.

The objective is to perform a comprehensive EDA to gain insights into various factors like price, mileage, engine capacity, etc., in the Indian car market.

After basic EDA, various visualizations and statistical analyses were conducted to understand trends and patterns in the data.
